--- layout: troubleshooting-guide description: Home Assistant Voice Preview Edition guideName: Re-installing the firmware ---

It is not usually necessary to re-install the firmware. Only follow this procedure if you have a good reason to do so. Normally, you receive an update notification for the Home Assistant Voice Preview Edition software, just like any other Home Assistant update.
