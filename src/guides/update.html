Automatic update notification

No special steps are needed to update Home Assistant Voice Preview Edition. If a new software version is available, you will see a notification in Home Assistant, like with any other update.

I receive no automic update notification

You will not receive the device firmware updates automatically if you adopted the device in the ESPHome add-on because you wanted to customize the device further. You will still be able to benefit from updates by recompiling your device.
